## Final Constitution Principles

Remember the core principles that should guide your continued work:

> Intelligence gains meaning only when grounded in physical interaction. Always consider the physical reality of your systems.

### **Simulation is Truth, Reality is the Test**
> Use simulation to accelerate learning, but validate everything in the real world.

### **Human Dignity Over Automation**
> Design systems that assist, not replace; augment, not dominate.
